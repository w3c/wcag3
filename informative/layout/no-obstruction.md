## User needs

* As a person with a cognitive disability, I need sites with content that scrolls over background ads to have the ability to turn these ads off or embed them inline.
* As a person with a cognitive disability, if an article or website is partially obscured with a call to action button to view more, it is clearly presented so users don’t click on the fake ad links instead.
* As a person with low vision, I need sites to not obstruct me or redirect me from completing a task or leaving the site.
