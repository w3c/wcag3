## User needs

* As a person who needs or prefers to use sign language instead of spoken language, I need a sign language alternative to audio. 
* As a person who needs sign language, I need important live and recorded audio content presented in my sign language (not only written captions) so I can fully understand it.
* As a person who uses sign language, I need to be able to turn any sign language on and off.
* As a person who uses sign language, I need any sign language version to accurately reflect the audio content.
* As a person who uses sign language, I need any sign language version to avoid obscuring any visual content.
* As a person who uses sign language, I need to be able to reposition and resize any sign language window so I can watch signer and content together.
* As a person who uses sign language, I need the signer to be framed so I can see head, face, hands and upper torso (no cropping) so signing remains intelligible.
* As a person who uses sign language, I need the interpretation to include prosody and facial expression to ensure the message is intelligible.
* As a person who uses sign language, I need the sign language to match the appropriate language for the geographic location of the page/view.
* As a person who uses sign language, I need to know that there is sign language interpretation available for audio content.
* As a person who speaks sign language, I need an easy, discoverable way to request a human sign-language interpreter for live events and customer support.
