## User needs

* As a person with a cognitive disability, I need to be able to read content without being distracted by background advertisements or other background elements.
* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need to be able to distinguish legitimate controls from advertisements and other deceptive content.
* As a person with low vision, I need websites not to obstruct or divert me from completing a task or leaving the site.
* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need to be able to remove distractions.
* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need all parts of a page to be available when it appears to have finished loading.
