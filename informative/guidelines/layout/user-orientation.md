## User needs

* As a person who is blind or has low vision, or a cognitive or learning disability, I need to know where I am within a process, page, or page section.
* As a person who is blind or has low vision, or a cognitive or learning disability, I need to understand where I am, even after switching away and returning to a page or view.
* As a person who is blind or has low vision, or a cognitive or learning disability, I need to know when content has changed or been updated, even if the changes happen quickly.
* As a person with a memory, attention, or executive function impairment, or as a person with a communication disability who uses symbols, I need to understand the purpose of the content, so that I can tell where I am and what I'm doing if I lose focus or become distracted.
* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need to recognize where I am within a website, application, or multi-step process, even after I become distracted.
* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need to understand how the current page or view relates to the overall website or task, even after I become distracted.
