## User needs

* As a person with a cognitive and learning disability, I need to be able to easily compare product features, costs, and other essential information.
* As a person who is blind, I need screen readers to present information without unnecessary or repetitive announcements.
* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need processes and content to be simple enough so that I'm not overwhelmed and can focus on the most essential information.
* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need only the options relevant to my current task to be prominent, while still being able to access additional features when I need them.
* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need content to be visually organized into understandable sections.
* As a person who is blind, I need to be able to distinguish different sections of content without relying on visual presentation.
* As a person who is blind or has a cognitive or learning disability, I need to understand the purpose of different sections of content.
* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need to understand how different sections of content relate to each other.
* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need visual spacing and layout to make content easy to distinguish so that sections are clear, the visual hierarchy is obvious, and the page is not overwhelming.
* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need to be able to interact with content without becoming overwhelmed or distracted by unrelated content.
* As a person who is blind, I need to be able to navigate directly to different sections of a long page.
* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need to understand that a page is organized into logical sections and the purpose of those sections.
* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need to understand the relationships between different parts of the content.
* As a person who is blind or has low vision, or a motor, cognitive or learning disability, I need content and interactions to be presented in a logical order.
* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need summaries to be presented in a logical order.
* As a person who is blind or has a motor disability, I need to be able to navigate content efficiently.
