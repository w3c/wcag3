## Tests

<i>Ensure that steps are identified and convey number or structure</i>

<b>Procedure</b>

For each multi-step process:
1. Determine whether the interaction consists of multiple sequential steps
2. Verify that the multi-step process is indicated in every step
3. Verify that this identification is perceivable by users
4. Verify that the way steps are identified conveys either:
  * the total number of steps, or
  * the structure of the process (e.g., sequence or grouping)

<b>Expected results</b>

#2, #3, and #4 are true
