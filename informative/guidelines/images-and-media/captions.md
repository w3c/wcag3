## User needs

* As a person who is deaf or hard of hearing, I need access to synchronized captions that are equivalent and equal in content to that of the audio, including identifying the speaker, sound effects and other significant audio.
* As a person who is deaf or hard of hearing I need to know when there is a change in language in a transcript or captions. 
* As a person who is deaf or hard of hearing, I need access to the auditory content through captions or transcripts in a different language than that of the media.
* As a user who is deaf or hard of hearing and has a cognitive disability, I need the ability to use enhanced captions to interact with the captions in a way that allows me to look up terms or identify things.
* As a person who is deaf or hard of hearing who uses extended reality, I need a way to access captions in 360 degree space.
* As a person who is deaf or hard of hearing, I need to be able to customize the size of my captions.
