## User needs

* As a person who is deaf or hard of hearing, I need access to synchronized captions that are equivalent in content to the audio, including speaker identification, sound effects, and other significant audio information.
* As a person who is deaf or hard of hearing, I need to know when there is a change in language in captions.
* As a person who is deaf or hard of hearing, I need access to auditory content through captions in a different language than that of the media.
* As a person who is deaf or hard of hearing and has a cognitive disability, I need enhanced captions that let me look up unfamiliar terms and identify referenced people, places or things.
* As a person who is deaf or hard of hearing and uses extended reality, I need a way to access captions in 360-degree space.
* As a person who is deaf or hard of hearing, I need to be able to customize the size of my captions.
