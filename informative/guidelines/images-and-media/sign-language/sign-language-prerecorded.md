## Tests

### Sign language for audio only

<b>Procedure</b>

For each instance of prerecorded audio content:
1. Sign language interpretation is available either as picture-in-picture or as a linked signed video closely associated with the audio controls. 
2. Sign language interpretation uses a version of sign language appropriate for the locale of the page/view.

<b>Expected results</b>
* #1 and #2 are true.
