## Tests

### Sign language equivalent

<b>Procedure</b>

For each instance of prerecorded audio content:
1. Select a sample totaling at least 5% (for example, 1.5 minutes from each 30 minutes) of audio that contains meaningful spoken content.
2. The meaning of the spoken content is conveyed accurately by the sign language interpretation.

<b>Expected results</b>
* #2 is true.
