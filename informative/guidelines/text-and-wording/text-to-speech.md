## User needs

* As a person who is blind, I need semantic text indicators to reflect their actual purpose rather than being used only for visual effect.
* As a person who is blind, I need to understand the meaning conveyed by text formatting and styling, such as strikethrough, inserted text, bold, italics, or capital letters.
* As a person who is blind and uses a screen reader, I need to be aware when the language of content changes.
* As a person who is blind, I need the information conveyed by the visual presentation of text to be available programmatically.
