## User needs

* As a person with a vestibular disorder, I need to be able to control audio effects that create the perception of motion.
* As a person with sensory sensitivities, I need to control haptic sensations to protect myself from overstimulation.
* As a person who has a cognitive or learning disability, I need to avoid content that might be triggering for me.
* As a person who is susceptible to seizures triggered by flashing content, I need to avoid digital content that flashes.
* As a person with mental health disabilities, with the exception of warnings about flashing content, I need to be able to choose which types of trigger warnings are shown to me.
* As a person with a mental health disability who may be adversely affected by distressing content, I need a warning before viewing content that may be disturbing.
* As a person with a vestibular disorder or motion  sensitivity, I need to be able to quickly stop or escape motion effects when they occur.
* As a person with a vestibular disorder or motion sensitivity, I need a warning before encountering motion effects, and an alternative way to complete the task.
* As a person with a vestibular disorder or motion sensitivity, I need to be able to complete a task without being affected by unrelated motion elsewhere in the content.
* As a person with a vestibular disorder, motion sensitivity or visual processing difficulties, I need to be able to control the effects of motion so that I can complete a flow.
* As a person with a vestibular disorder, motion sensitivity or visual processing difficulties, I need help resuming where I left off if motion interrupts my task.
* As a person with motion sensitivity, sensory sensitivities, or visual processing difficulties, I need to be able to reduce motion effects that occur while browsing content, such as during scrolling.
