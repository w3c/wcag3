## User needs

* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need to understand which information is optional or not be asked for unnecessary information.
* As a person with a motor disability, I need to be able to complete tasks without unnecessary repeated actions.
* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need purchasing processes to be as simple as possible.
* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need processes and content to be simple so that I'm not overwhelmed and can focus on the most essential information.
* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need instructions to include only the information necessary to complete my task.
* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need to be able to interact with content without becoming overwhelmed or distracted by unrelated content.
