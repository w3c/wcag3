## User needs

* As a person with a mental health disability, I need to be able to manage the amount of time I spend on different types of internet use.
* As a person with anxiety, I need to be able to complete tasks without being stressed by countdown timers so that I do not make mistakes.
* As a person who has a motor, cognitive or learning disability, I need enough time to complete tasks requested by the user interface.
* As a person who has a motor, cognitive or learning disability, I need to know how much time I have to complete a timed process.
* As a person who has a motor, cognitive or learning disability, I need to be able to take breaks while completing a process.
* As a person who has a motor, cognitive or learning disability, I need enough time to complete a task before it times out.
* As a person with a cognitive, learning, or mental health disability, I need to be able to complete tasks without unnecessary pressure or urgency.
* As a person who has a motor, cognitive or learning disability, I need to be able to extend or disable timeouts.
* As a person who experiences sensory overload or visual processing difficulties from moving content, I need to be able to avoid motion in visual countdown timers.
