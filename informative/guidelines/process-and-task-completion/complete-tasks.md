## User needs

* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need to understand the level of effort required to complete a process.
* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need to know what information I need to complete a process.
* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need to know what tools I will need to complete a process.
* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need to understand when a control or input is required to move to the next step in a process.
* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need to understand my options and the actions I can take, and identify the controls I can use to complete them.
* As a person with a cognitive or learning disability, I need instructions to remain available in a predictable location while I complete a task.
