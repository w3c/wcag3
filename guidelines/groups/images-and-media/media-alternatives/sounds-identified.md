Sounds needed to understand the media are identified or described in :term[captions] and :term[transcripts].

:::ednote
This includes sound effects and other non-spoken :term[audio] :term[content].
:::

:::except-when
It is a decorative video.
:::

:::applies-when
there is sound.
:::

:::tests
<i>Meaningful sounds in captions</i>

<b>Procedure</b>
For all audio content: 
2. Identify meaningful non-verbal audio (sounds).
3. Check that captions include a description of the meaningful non-verbal audio.

<b>Expected results</b>
* #3 is true.
:::
