Parts of graphical object required to understand the content meet a minimum :term[contrast ratio test]

:::except-when
* a particular presentation of graphical objects is essential to the information being conveyed.


:::tests
<b>Procedure</b>

For each graphical object:
1. Identify all parts of the graphical objects that convey information.
2. Confirm that these parts meet the minimum contrast ratio with adjacent colors.


<b>Expected results</b>
* #2 is true.

:::
