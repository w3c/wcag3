title: Captions centered (immersive)
---

In 360-degree digital environments, :term[captions] remain directly in front of the user.

:::applies-when
- the position of the captions is controlled by the user.
:::

:::tests
<b>Procedure</b>

For each caption in 360-degree environments:
1. Check that captions remain directly in front of the user.

<b>Expected results</b>
* #1 is true.
:::
