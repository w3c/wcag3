A :term[mechanism] is available to turn :term[sign language interpretation] on and off. 

:::except-when
- Sign language interpretation is hard-coded into the video content.
- A sign language interpreted version is provided as a separate video.
:::

:::example
Hard-coded sign language interpretation would include video content where the interpreter is standing next to the speaker in the video.
:::
