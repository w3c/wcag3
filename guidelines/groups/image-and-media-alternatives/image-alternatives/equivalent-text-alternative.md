Equivalent :term[text alternatives] are available for :term[images] that convey :term[content].

:::ednote
* Method: Provide a text alternative for an image that conveys meaning or content.
:::

:::tests
Provide a text alternative for the image in a way that conveys the equivalent meaning or content that's displayed visually.

<i>(General) Equivalent text alternative</i>

<b>Procedure</b>
1. Remove, hide, or mask the image.
2. Replace it with the text alternative.
3. Check that the meaningful content in the image is described by the text alternative).
4. If the image contains words that are important to understanding the content, check that those words are included in the text alternative.

<b>Expected results</b>
* #3 is true. 
* #4 is also true, if the image contains words that are important to understanding the content.
:::
