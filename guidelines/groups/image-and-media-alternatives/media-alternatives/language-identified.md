title: Speaker language identified
---

When more than one language is spoken in :term[audio] :term[content], the language spoken by each speaker is identified in all :term[media alternatives].

:::except-when
Incidental word usage.
:::

:::tests
<i>Language identified in transcripts</i>

<b>Procedure</b>
1. Identify all audio or video transcripts within the unit of conformance that include multiple languages.
2. Changes in language are identified in the media alternatives.

<b>Expected results</b>
* #2 is true.
:::
