The timing and positioning of notifications and other interruptions can be changed, suppressed or saved, except interruptions involving an emergency.

A :term[mechanism] is available to allow users to change the timing and placement of notifications and other interruptions, or to suppress or save them.

:::applies-when
Notifications or other interruptions are present.
:::

:::except-when
Interruptions involve an emergency or are essential.
:::

:::tests
<b>Procedure</b>

1. When interruptions are present, check that a mechanism is available to change the timing and placement of notifications, or to suppress or save them.

<b>Expected Results</b>

* #1 is true
:::
