During the completion of a :term[process], preselected options that impact finance, privacy or safety are visibly and programmatically available to the user, by default.

1. Identify all options that affect finance, privacy and safety.

3. For all identified in Step 1, verify that they are programmatically available before completing the process.

<b>Expected results</b>
* #2 is true.
* #3 is true.
