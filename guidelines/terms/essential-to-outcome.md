always necessary to achieve the same result

:::note
If something is essential to the outcome then: If it were removed, the information or functionality of the content would be fundamentally changed, and the information and functionality cannot be achieved in another way that would conform
:::

:::example
* Content and/or markup that fails because it is an example of failure or a test sample to be used testing a tool.
* Real-time based exceptions such as:
  * purchasing items in limited supply;
  * a dutch auction where the price keeps dropping until the first person hits the BUY button;
  * controlling a drone in flight;
  * submitting a proposal by deadline.
* There can be no ability to undo, such as purchasing things in an auction.
:::
